Nicholas Gati, a composer & sound designer from New York, founded AllaMusica LLC in 2013. He holds a Bachelors Degree in Music Composition from Five Towns College and received the Music Composition Award upon graduation. He has also studied composition privately with composers from The Juilliard School, The Shepherd School of Music at Rice University and Five Towns College.

Nicholas Gati is affiliated with ASCAP and is a member of the Long Island Composers Alliance (LICA).
