AllaMusica LLC

AllaMusica (it), 'alla [prep.+ art.], 'mu·zi·ka [n]: To The Music

AllaMusica LLC is a boutique music composition and audio post company. We are a network of composers, producers, engineers and sound designers ready to give the breath of life to your productions.

“Where words fail, music speaks” , once stated by Hans Christian Andersen, has become an integral part of both our philosophy and mission at AllaMusica. We strive to enhance our clients’ visual and audible productions with custom music that will speak directly to our clients' target market through focused and purposeful harmony and sound.
